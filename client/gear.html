Avalanche Transceiver



A digital three-antenna device reduced to the essential functions. The Barryvox® scores well on simple navigation and clear instructions. With a large receiving bandwidth and an effective search strip width of 70 meters, it offers users a simple interface and intuitive operation. From an easy-to-read display to buttons that can be operated even wearing gloves, shock-proof and break-proof housing and a revolutionarily simple visual interface, the Barryvox® shows that powerful performance and sophisticated search guidance do not need to be complicated.

Avalanche Shovel



A savior in time of need: the high-performance tiltproof design of the Alugator Pro Light blade, together with the ergonomically shaped D-grip, offers maximum stability and transfer of strength when shoveling. The variable positioning of the shaft also allows the Alugator Pro to be used as a 90° pick and completes your safety equipment as a reliable shovel.

Avalanche Probe



Light, bend-resistant and reliable: the Carbon Probe 280 fast lock features a specially developed Fast Lock system for quick and effective operation. The extremely precise probe scaling on both sides allows you to exactly determine the depth of the probe in the snow. Length 280 cm.

Avalanche Airbag



Equipped with our Removable Airbag System 3.0, the Pro X is our absolute all-arounder: whether freeriding or on one-day ski and snowboard tours, this backpack sits comfortably on your back and is at your side in dangerous situations: Safety first! The high-performance and highly functional backpack raises the bar in terms of comfort, material used, construction and clever features. The fully opening rear zipper allows generous access to the main compartment, even when a snowboard is attached to the front. Inside, there is also a molded, soft-lined compartment for your ski goggles. The main material is completely recycled. A backpack, whose technical features meet every possible safety requirement.

Airbag Cartridge



The cartridge is an essential component of the avalanche airbag system and must be ordered separately. It can be refilled by the user after any deployment using a compressor or diving cylinder. The filling pressure of the cartridge can be checked quickly and safely using a pressure gauge. The Cartridge Refill Kit and a compressed air source are required for refilling.
